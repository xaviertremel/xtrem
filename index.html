Adkicker

I have an online marketing background and have been working with a colleague of mine on a online tool that helps marketers to quickly create targeted paid marketing campaigns: adkicker.io

The tool allows you to automatically generate keywords and ads based on the products or services that your website is selling.

Adwords Scripts

I developed adwords scripts that allows to programmaticaly perform actions on the campaigns. This is particularly relevant for e-commerce websites that have a lot of products to advertise.

Examples of Scripts:

  • Pausing campaigns according to inventory
  • Decreasing budget when the stock is low
  • Increasing the bids when products are on sale
